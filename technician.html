Install MVP Tech App
Add this app to your iPhone home screen for the full experience - works offline, feels native.
1
Tap the Share button at the bottom of Safari - it looks like a box with an arrow pointing up ⬆
2
Scroll down and tap Add to Home Screen
3
Tap Add in the top-right corner
4
Open the MVP Tech icon from your home screen - you're done!
Must be opened in Safari - Chrome and other browsers on iPhone don't support home screen install.
Install the app to your Android home screen for quick access and a native feel.
1
Tap Install Now below - your browser will prompt you to add it to your home screen.
1
Tap the ⋮ menu (three dots) in the top-right of Chrome
2
Tap Add to Home screen
3
Tap Add to confirm
MVP Tech can be installed as a desktop app in Chrome or Edge.
1
Look for the install icon (⊕) in your browser's address bar
2
Click it and select Install
